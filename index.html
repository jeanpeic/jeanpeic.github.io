Is cinema dead?

New tools give us new ways to tell and experience stories, and I wonder what the future of cinema might look like. Hopefully, it's a future where more people can tell their own stories. Where those stories are shared, valued, and maybe even help us reimagine the world around us.

My research with Prof. Maneesh Agrawala aims to nudge cinema in this direction, ever so slightly. More broadly, I'm interested in creating spaces where the worlds of technology and film can meet and discuss these things.
